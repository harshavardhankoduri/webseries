ATTACK ON TITAN ANIME

ATTACK ON TITAN

Attack on Titan tells the story of a world where humans live inside massive walled cities to protect themselves from giant man-eating Titans. As the walls are breached, a young boy named Eren Yeager vows to destroy all Titans, only to uncover hidden truths about the origin of Titans and the real enemies threatening humanity.

SEASONS
SEASON 1

Attack on Titan Season 1 follows Eren Yeager, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armin Arlert after their hometown is destroyed by the sudden appearance of the Colossal and Armored Titans. Eren joins the military to fight back and later discovers he can transform into a Titan himself, leading to intense battles and the start of uncovering dark secrets about the Titans and the walls protecting humanity.

SEASON 2

Attack on Titan Season 2 focuses on uncovering the mystery behind the Titans and the people within the walls. As new Titans appear and secrets are revealed, Eren and his friends learn that some of their own comrades—like Reiner and Bertholdt—are actually Titan shifters, leading to intense battles and shocking betrayals.

SEASON 3

Season 3 of Attack on Titan, the world unites to stop Eren Yeager, who unleashes the Rumbling—a devastating march of colossal Titans—to annihilate humanity outside Paradis Island. Former friends and foes, including Mikasa, Armin, and Reiner, join forces to confront Eren in a desperate bid to end the destruction. The battle is emotionally intense, filled with sacrifice, and ultimately forces them to choose between loyalty, survival, and the fate of the world.
